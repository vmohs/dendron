i give you my body–
so you love me, and you agree
with all the ridiculous things i say.

in this room full of people
who look like me,
i don't belong.

i can't help but think i'd be
more agreeable if i had just
kissed you back.

but i don't want to. and
you don't want it. and
the threat of my absence looms.

but no one cares,
or shows it, dare to utter
my name out loud?

do you hate the way
it sounds, feels on your tongue?
venus, venus, venus

i should change my name
again, deconstruct myself,
again, stay home alone

again. burning hot, sulfuric,
unclad / raw;
this is how i am loved.

in twos, not in
threes and especially not
in the living room.
