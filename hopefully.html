it’s easy to lose yourself
when you don’t know who
you are or if it even matters.
i closed the door, baby,
did you remember to lock it
behind you? because i’m haunting
you and you have to choose–
me? or the ghost? i want you
to have questions
for me because
i have answers. i was there too.
i remember it well.

the entire narrative is possessed
by You

when the dust finally settled
i traced everything
back to the day of the
bisection. are you there?
do you know if ashes
can become the paint? i just can’t
help myself. paint is so
exposing.

here, i made this for you.
this is what you mean to me.
this is how i see you.
in this light, in these colors.
this is how i write you,
with these words,
in this rhythm.

scraps of paper tucked inside pillows,
patches of fabric,
torn canvases and drawings
gathering dust
beneath the bed, flowers pressed
between sketchbook pages, a
handmade necklace, removed upon
autopsy. alone, with hope.
or hoping to be alone.
